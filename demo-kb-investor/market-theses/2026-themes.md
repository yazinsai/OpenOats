# Investment Theses — 2026

---

## Thesis: Vertical AI Agents Replace SaaS Seats
**When they say:** AI agent, vertical AI, replacing jobs, automating workflows, domain-specific AI

- The next wave is AI agents that replace entire $80K+ job functions in specific verticals
- Want: founders with 10+ years domain expertise, not AI researchers who picked a vertical
- Want: workflows where errors are expensive (healthcare, legal, finance, construction)
- **Anti-pattern:** "We can apply this to any industry" — if they can't name the specific person whose job changes, they don't have a thesis
- **Anti-pattern:** "ChatGPT wrapper for X" — no defensibility
- Portfolio fit: Driftwood (construction), Canopy Health (healthcare), Arable (agriculture)

---

## Thesis: Local-First AI for Privacy-Sensitive Verticals
**When they say:** on-premise, local AI, data privacy, HIPAA, SOX, can't send data to cloud, on-device

- Cloud AI won't work for healthcare, finance, legal, government
- Winners in these verticals will run models on-premise or on-device
- **Why now:** Apple Silicon makes on-device inference practical — models under 8B parameters run well on M-series
- Want: technical founders who understand model optimization
- Want: business model not dependent on usage-based cloud compute
- Portfolio fit: OpenOats (local transcription + KB)

---

## Thesis: Infrastructure for the AI Agent Economy
**When they say:** agent infrastructure, picks and shovels, agent-to-agent, agent monitoring, agent compliance

- As AI agents proliferate, they need infrastructure: identity, payments, monitoring, testing, compliance
- Want: auth/permissions for agent-to-agent communication
- Want: observability and debugging for agent workflows
- **Anti-pattern:** "We're building an agent" — we have enough agents, we need agent infrastructure
- Portfolio fit: Kindling (agent docs), Tessera (agent QA — stretch)

## Valuation Guardrails
**When they say:** valuation, how much are you raising, post-money, pre-money

| Stage | Revenue | Acceptable Valuation |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| Pre-seed | Pre-revenue | $5-8M post |
| Seed | $10-50K MRR | $10-20M post |
| Seed | $50-100K MRR | $15-25M post |
| Series A | $100K+ MRR, clear PMF | $30-60M post |

- **Red line:** Never pay >40x ARR at seed
- **Series A:** >25x requires exceptional net retention (>150%)
- Pre-revenue at $25M+ post = almost always a pass
