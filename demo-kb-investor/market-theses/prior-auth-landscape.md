# Prior Authorization AI — Competitors

**When they say:** prior auth, no competition in prior auth, nobody doing AI prior authorization, utilization management

This space has over $180M in total funding. Anyone claiming "no competition" is wrong.

---

## Cohere Health — $106M raised (Series C)
- AI-powered prior auth for **payers** (insurance companies), not providers
- Processes **50M+ prior auth decisions/year**
- Contracts with health plans covering **30M+ lives**
- Moat: 5 years of clinical decision data for model training

---

## Infinitus Health — $53M raised (Series B, a16z)
- AI voice agents that **make prior auth phone calls** on behalf of providers
- Handles **500K+ calls/month** — literally calls insurers and waits on hold
- Moat: proprietary phone conversation AI trained on millions of payer interactions

---

## Rhyme Health — $12M raised (Seed, General Catalyst)
- Prior auth automation for **specialty medications**
- ~20 specialty pharmacy partners
- Focused where denial rates are **30%+** and each denial costs **$2K+ in rework**

---

## Valer Health — $8.5M raised (Seed)
- Real-time prior auth prediction **at the point of care**
- Pilot with 3 health systems
- Same angle as most new entrants — doctor orders procedure, system predicts approval

---

## Olive AI → Waystar
- Raised **$900M+** before imploding; prior auth product line survived
- Waystar acquired prior auth assets in 2024
- Lesson: proves the market is real but "AI for everything in healthcare" doesn't work

---

## Key Questions for New Entrants
- Selling to payers or providers? Completely different sales motion
- What clinical data advantage? Cohere has 5 years of decision data
- How do you handle the long tail of payer-specific rules?
- CMS mandate is a tailwind but Epic/Cerner will build basic automation — how do you stay ahead?
