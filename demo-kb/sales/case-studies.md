# Customer Case Studies

## Meridian Capital (Financial Services, 120 seats)

**Problem:** Investment committee meetings produced inconsistent notes. Junior analysts spent 3-4 hours post-meeting reconstructing decisions and action items. Critical context from prior meetings was frequently lost.

**Solution:** Deployed OpenOats with a KB containing their investment thesis templates, portfolio company summaries, and due diligence checklists. Real-time suggestions surfaced relevant portfolio data during live discussions.

**Results:**
- Meeting follow-up time reduced from 3.5 hours to 40 minutes (81% reduction)
- Decision documentation improved — audit found 94% of key decisions captured vs. 61% before
- "The suggestions caught a contradictory assumption about TAM sizing that would have cost us. That alone paid for the annual contract." — Managing Director

## Recurve Health (Healthcare SaaS, 45 seats)

**Problem:** Clinical advisory board meetings required real-time reference to FDA guidance, prior study results, and regulatory timelines. Participants frequently made claims that contradicted their own documentation.

**Solution:** KB loaded with regulatory filings, clinical trial summaries, and board minutes from the past 2 years.

**Results:**
- Regulatory compliance incidents in board discussions dropped from ~2/quarter to 0
- Meeting duration decreased 22% — less time spent looking up references
- "It's like having a regulatory expert whispering in your ear during every meeting." — VP Clinical Affairs

## Alloy Ventures (VC, 18 seats)

**Problem:** Partners couldn't remember details from 200+ founder pitches per year. Pattern recognition across portfolio was ad-hoc and inconsistent.

**Solution:** KB populated with pitch notes, market maps, and portfolio company updates. During new pitches, the system surfaces connections to existing portfolio companies and prior market analysis.

**Results:**
- 3x increase in cross-portfolio introductions (suggestions surfaced non-obvious connections)
- Due diligence cycle shortened by 4 days on average
- Partners report feeling "30% more prepared" in follow-up meetings
