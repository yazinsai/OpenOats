# Pricing & Packaging

## Current Plans

| Plan | Price | Seats | Features |
|------|-------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $0/mo | 1 | 5 meetings/mo, basic transcription |
| Pro | $29/mo | 1 | Unlimited meetings, KB suggestions, export |
| Team | $22/seat/mo | 5-50 | Everything in Pro + shared KB, admin controls |
| Enterprise | Custom | 50+ | SSO, audit logs, dedicated support, on-prem KB |

## Discounting Rules

- Annual billing: 20% discount (show monthly equivalent)
- Never discount below $18/seat on Team
- Enterprise floor: $15/seat at 200+ seats
- Competitors switching: offer 3 months free on annual
- No mid-cycle refunds; credit toward upgrade only

## Common Objections

**"Too expensive compared to Otter/Fireflies"**
Those tools are transcription-only. Our KB suggestion engine is unique — no competitor offers real-time contextual coaching during meetings. The ROI comes from better meeting outcomes, not cheaper transcription.

**"We already use Gong/Chorus"**
Gong is post-meeting analysis for sales managers. We're in-meeting support for the person actually talking. They're complementary, not competing. Several customers run both.

**"Can we get a pilot?"**
Yes — 14-day Team trial, no credit card. If they need longer, max 30 days with VP approval. Never extend beyond 30 days; it kills urgency.

## Upsell Triggers

- Customer mentions "team" or "multiple users" → pitch Team plan
- Customer mentions "compliance" or "security" → pitch Enterprise
- Customer asks about API → Enterprise only, mention webhook integrations
- Customer mentions "CRM" → Enterprise has Salesforce/HubSpot integration
