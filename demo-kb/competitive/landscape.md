# Competitive Landscape — March 2026

## Direct Competitors

### Granola
- **Positioning:** "AI notepad for meetings"
- **Strengths:** Beautiful UI, good brand, strong content marketing
- **Weaknesses:** No real-time suggestions, no KB integration, notes-only (no coaching)
- **Pricing:** $12/mo individual, $10/seat team
- **Our advantage:** We do what Granola does (transcription + notes) PLUS real-time KB-backed suggestions. They're a notepad; we're a copilot.

### Otter.ai
- **Positioning:** "AI meeting assistant"
- **Strengths:** Strong brand recognition, good integrations, mobile app
- **Weaknesses:** Cloud-only (privacy concerns), no local processing, generic suggestions without KB grounding
- **Pricing:** $17/mo Pro, $25/seat Business
- **Our advantage:** Local-first privacy model, KB-grounded suggestions (not generic AI), macOS-native quality

### Fireflies.ai
- **Positioning:** "AI notetaker"
- **Strengths:** Wide integration ecosystem, conversation intelligence features
- **Weaknesses:** Cloud-only, focused on post-meeting analysis, no real-time coaching
- **Pricing:** $19/mo Pro, $29/seat Business
- **Our advantage:** Real-time vs. post-hoc. We help you during the meeting, not after.

## Adjacent Players (Not Direct Competitors)

### Gong / Chorus (now ZoomInfo)
- Revenue intelligence platforms for sales teams
- Post-meeting analysis, deal scoring, coaching recommendations
- $100-150/seat/mo — different price tier entirely
- Complementary to OpenOats: Gong for manager coaching, OpenOats for rep in-meeting support

### Notion AI / Mem
- Knowledge management with AI search
- Could theoretically add meeting features but haven't
- Our KB is meeting-context-aware; theirs is general-purpose search

## Defensibility

1. **Local-first architecture:** Competitors are cloud-only. Privacy-sensitive orgs (finance, healthcare, legal) can only choose us.
2. **Real-time KB suggestions:** No competitor does this. Otter has "AI chat" but it's post-meeting and not grounded in user documents.
3. **macOS-native quality:** System audio capture, screen share hiding, menu bar integration — web apps can't match this.
4. **Embedding + reranking pipeline:** Our Voyage AI reranking produces measurably better KB matches than cosine-only search.
